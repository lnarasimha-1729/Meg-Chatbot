[UC-041]
Q: What is the Focus scheme?
A: Focus is the Meghalaya state scheme for forming and financing Producer Groups (PGs). Each PG receives seed capital in tranches and is linked to an Integrated Village Cooperative Society (IVCS). It is distinct from FOCUS+ (Direct Benefit Transfer).

----------------------------------------------------------------------

[UC-042]
Q: How is Focus different from FOCUS+?
A: **Focus** = Producer Group formation + finance (37k PGs). **FOCUS+** = Direct Benefit Transfer (₹5,000/yr) to individual farmers (92k beneficiaries). Different schemes, different beneficiary lists.

----------------------------------------------------------------------

[UC-043]
Q: What is a bookkeeper in the Focus scheme?
A: Each PG is required to identify one member as bookkeeper — responsible for tracking PG receipts, expenses, and member contributions. Bookkeeper identification is a precondition for tranche-2 disbursement.

----------------------------------------------------------------------

[UC-044]
Q: What is IVCS?
A: Integrated Village Cooperative Society — the legal entity Focus PGs are nested under at village level. Acts as the financial conduit for tranche disbursements.

----------------------------------------------------------------------

[UC-045]
Q: What are the Focus disbursement tranches?
A: Tranche 1: initial seed capital (~₹49k avg). Tranche 2: follow-on disbursement, contingent on bookkeeper identification and tranche-1 utilisation report. Currently only 10 PGs have received tranche 2.
